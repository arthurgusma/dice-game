HOW TO PLAY:

  • The active player has to roll the dice.
  • The sum of the rollings is added to the current score.
  • When the player decides to stop rolling, he should click on 'HOLD' and his current score gets added to his Global Score.
  • It's next player turn.
  • Try to get to 100 before you mate.
